# 重要极限

## 基本重要极限

$$\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow0}\frac{\sin(x)}{x}=1$$
$$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow\infty}\left( 1+\frac{k}{x} \right)^x=e^{k}$$
$$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow0}(1+kx)^{\frac{1}{x}}=e^{k}$$

## 其他极限
$$\begin{cases}lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{\tan(x)}{x}=1\\\\ \lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{\arcsin(x)}{x}=1\\\\ \lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{\arctan(x)}{x}=1\\\\ \lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{1-\cos(x)}{x^{2}}=\frac{1}{2}\\\\\lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{e^{x}-1}{x}=1\\\\\lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{a^{x}-1}{x}=\ln(a)\quad (a>0,a\ne1)\\\\\lim_{x \rightarrow0} \frac{\ln(1+x)}{x}=1\end{cases}$$

# 等价无穷小

- 当 $x \rightarrow0$ 时，有以下等价关系
$$\begin{cases}\sin(x)\sim x\\\\\tan(x)\sim x\\\\ \arcsin(x)\sim x\\\\ \arctan(x)\sim x\\\\ e^{x}-1\sim x\\\\ \ln(1+x)\sim x\\\\ a^{x}-1 \sim x\ln(a)\\\\ (1+x)^{a}-1 \sim ax\\\\ 1-\cos(x)\sim \frac{1}{2} x^{2}\\\\ x-\sin(x)\sim \frac{1}{6}x^{3}\\\\ \tan(x)-x\sim \frac{1}{3 }x^{3}\end{cases}$$

# 常见导数

$$\begin{cases}(c)'=0\\\\(x^{n})'=nx^{n-1}\\\\(e^{x})'=e^{x}\\\\(a^{x})'=a^{x}\ln(a)\\\\(\ln(x))'=\frac{1}{x}\\\\(\log_{a}(x))'= \frac{1}{x\ln(a)}\\\\(\sin(x))'=\cos(x)\\\\(\cos(x))'=-\sin(x)\\\\(\tan(x))'=\sec^{2}(x)\\\\(\cot(x))'=-\csc^{2}(x)\\\\(\sec(x))'=\sec(x)\tan(x)\\\\(\csc(x))'=-\csc(x)\cot(x)\\\\(\arcsin(x))'= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}\\\\(\arccos(x))'=- \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}\\\\(\arctan(x))'= \frac{1}{1+x^{2}}\\\\(arccot(x))'=- \frac{1}{1+x^{2}}\end{cases}$$

# 常见积分

$$\begin{cases}k\sim kx+C\\\\x^{n}\sim \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C\\\\ \frac{1}{x}\sim \ln|x|+C\\\\e^{x} \sim e^{x}+C\\\\a^{x}\sim \frac{a^{x}}{\ln a}+C\\\\\cos(x)\sim \sin(x)+C\\\\\sin(x)\sim -\cos(x)+C\\\\ \sec^{2}(x)\sim tan(x)+C\\\\\csc^{2}(x)\sim-\cot(x)+C\\\\ \sec(x)\tan(x)\sim \sec(x)+C\\\\\csc(x)\cot(x)\sim -\csc(x)+C\\\\ \tan(x)\sim \ln|\sec(x)|+C\\\\\cot(x)\sim \ln|\sin(x)|+C\\\\\sec(x)\sim\ln|\sec(x)+\tan(x)|+C\\\\\csc(x)\sim \ln|\csc(x)-\cot(x)|+C\\\\ \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^{2}-x^{2}}}=\arcsin\left(  \frac{x}{a} \right)+C\\\\ \frac{1}{a^{2}+x^{2}}\sim \frac{1}{a}\arctan\left(  \frac{x}{a} \right)+C\\\\ \frac{1}{x\sqrt{x^{2}-a^{2}}}\sim \frac{1}{a} \mathbf{arcsec}\left(  \frac{|x|}{a} \right)+C\end{cases}$$

## 常见定积分

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi} $$
$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-ax^2} dx = \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{a}} \quad (a > 0) $$
$$ \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{\sin(x)}{x} dx = \frac{\pi}{2} $$

- 下式：
	- 当 $n$ 是偶数时，结果为 $\frac{(n-1)!!}{n!!}\cdot \frac{\pi}{2}$
	- 当 $n$ 是奇数时，结果为 $\frac{(n-1)!!}{n!!}\cdot$

$$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin^n(x) dx = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \cos^n(x) dx $$
$$ \int_0^{2\pi} \sin(mx) \cos(nx) dx = 0 $$ $$ \int_0^{2\pi} \sin(mx) \sin(nx) dx = \pi \delta_{mn} $$ $$ \int_0^{2\pi} \cos(mx) \cos(nx) dx = \pi \delta_{mn} $$
- 其中
$$\delta_{mn}= \begin{cases}1\quad m=n\\\\0\quad m\ne n\end{cases}$$
